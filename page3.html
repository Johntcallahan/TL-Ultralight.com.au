Story of the Stream

As soon as you sit inside one of our Streams you will get to experience the fighter jet pilot feeling. Tandem seating and sophisticated outline of the aircraft, state of the art glass cockpit and stick furnished with many control devices are just a few features that stand behind the impeccable design, aerodynamic purity and precision in steering and control. Robust landing gear, ROTAX 912iS engine and the PowerMax propeller can only mean the following - safety, high speed, and the incredible experience of flying! The Stream is the future. The Stream is setting a new trend in ultralight flying.
